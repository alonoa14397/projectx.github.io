About BandPro

Band Pro Film Video Inc. was established in the USA in 1984, adjacent to Hollywood, California, to cater to the film and television market.

Today, Band Pro primarily sells equipment for the television and professional video production industries worldwide. Band Pro has several branches, including the Israeli branch located in Tel Aviv (60 Anilevich Street) near the Cinematheque parking lot.

Since the establishment of the Israeli branch in 1995, our focus has been on providing comprehensive solutions to supply the appropriate equipment while offering optimal conditions for project production.

Our professional team strives to bring a diverse, up-to-date, and high-quality range of equipment tailored to the needs of the evolving market. We offer a wide and updated selection of products from leading companies.
